Civil Engineer

Civil engineering is a professional engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical and naturally built environment, including public works such as roads, bridges, canals, dams, airports, sewage systems, pipelines, structural components of buildings, and railways

Computer Science

Computer science is the study of computers and computing as well as
their theoretical and practical applications. Computer science
applies the principles of mathematics, engineering, and logic to a
plethora of functions, including algorithm formulation, software
and hardware development, and artificial intelligence

Literature

Literature broadly is any collection of written work, but it is
also used more narrowly for writings specifically considered to be an
art form, especially prose fiction, drama, and poetry.[2] In recent
centuries, the definition has expanded to include oral literature, much
of which has been transcribed.[3] Literature is a method of recording,
preserving, and transmitting knowledge and entertainment, and can also
have a social, psychological, spiritual, or political role.
